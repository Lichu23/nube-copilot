# NubeCopilot

NubeCopilot is an AI assistant for Tiendanube store owners.

It helps you understand what is happening in your store by turning your sales, products, customers, and stock data into clear answers.

## What you can do

- Connect your Tiendanube store
- Ask questions about sales, orders, products, and inventory
- See charts, summaries, and alerts
- Save useful reports for later
- Review what changed over time

## Example questions

- Which products sold the most this week?
- Why did revenue go up or down?
- Which items are low on stock?
- What changed compared with last week?
- What should I focus on next?

## What the app is for

NubeCopilot is designed to help you make faster decisions without digging through spreadsheets.

It is read-only:

- it does not change your store
- it does not edit products or orders
- it only reads the data needed to answer your questions

## Privacy and access

Each store is kept separate.

That means:

- only the people allowed for a store can see its data
- your information is stored securely
- access can be revoked from your Tiendanube account

## Getting started

1. Open the app
2. Connect your Tiendanube store
3. Wait for the first sync
4. Start asking questions

## Need help?

If something looks wrong, check that your store is connected and fully synced.
